Math Genie Customer Agreement for {{ name }}
Enrollment in Math Genie: When you enroll your child into the Math Genie program, you commit to the program for the first four full months. If you decide to discontinue before the first four full months, you will still be liable to payments for remaining duration. If you wish to discontinue after the 4th month a 30 day notice is still required. (See Discontinuation)
Discontinuation: If you wish to discontinue, you must let us know no later than the 1st of the previous month. If proper notice is not given,payment will go through, and you will be charged for the month. Email and verbal notices are not sufficient. You must fill out a physical discontinuation form at your Math Genie center.
Breaks: If you wish to take a break, we must know by the 15th of the previous month. Email and verbal notices are not sufficient. You must fill out a break form in the Math Genie center. There will be no exceptions made. Breaks cannot exceed 30 days in a calendar year because extended breaks will cause disruptions in your child’s learning.
Math Genie locations are not day care centers. We do not provide child care services. Parents are responsible to drop off and pick up their children after every scheduled class. You must drop off and pick up your child from the center and not from outside in the parking lot. Please ensure that you are on time for your child’s scheduled class. We do not provide extra time for tardiness.
Late Pickup Fee You agree to pick up your child right after class dismissal. We will charge a late pick up fee of $15 for every 15 minute delay after class dismissal. This charge will be added to your bill. This is not a profit center for us. We rather not charge you this fee. So please pick up your child after class dismissal to avoid this fee.
Payment: Math Genie tuition is due on the 1st of each month. All Payments received after the 15th will be charged a $35 late fee. All unpaid accounts will be handed over to collections. You agree to pay all collection and attorney fees for late payments. All returned checks,ACH payments or declined credit card payments will incur a return fee of $35 after the 15th of month. Credit card charge backs will incur a penalty of $50. No refunds or credits will be given for unused classes or makeups.
Discounts: You may avail of special discounts for prepaying the tuition fees for the entire year. If you select the prepaid / discounted option, you agree that no refunds or credits will be given for unused classes under any circumstances.
Allergies: Math Genie facilities are not free of allergens. Many students bring snacks for their break time, and their foods may contain ingredients that your child may be allergic to. Parents concerned with food allergies must be aware of this risk.If your child has a severe allergy, please let us know, and efforts will be made to advise the class.Awesome Kid LLC DBA Math Genie, its affiliates and successors, etc. cannot assume any liability for adverse reactions to food consumed, or items one may come in contact with while present at a Math Genie location. Students with life threatening food allergies who may need to use an epi-pen should be carrying their own. Parents should wait in our waiting area to administer the epi-pen if that need arises. Math Genie staff are NOT trained to administer epi-pens and CANNOT provide or administer them.
Liability: You, as the parent/guardian, will hereby be liable for any and all damage/injury caused by your child. This includes, but is not limited to, the Math Genie center, its instructors, and its customers (children and adults).
Photo Waiver: You hereby give Awesome Kid LLC, its legal representatives, and assigns, the right to take, use, reuse, and publish a picture or video of your child in a Math Genie setting. This picture may be used to showcase your child’s success online. Awesome Kid LLC will never sell a photograph or video of your child to a third party.
Copyright: Math Genie materials are copyrighted and therefore subject to the U.S copyright laws. Any person found reproducing our material will be prosecuted by the fullest extent of the law.
Makeup Classes: If you are going to miss a class for any reason, you must inform us via email 24 hours in advance. In this situation, we may be able to give you a makeup class (if our schedule allows it). You will not be refunded for any missed classes. If you do not let us know in advance, we may not be able to give you a makeup class. Makeup classes will not be provided for absences that do not have advance notice. Makeups don't carry over. You must claim your makeup classes within 30 days.
You agree to not solicit Math Genie employees for any personal tutoring or any other employment. You agree to reimburse Math Genie for any loss of revenue for any violation.
Math Genie does not sell your information to any third party. You will not use the Math Genie premises to solicit our customers to your own personal business.
We are not a child or a dependent expense, and therefore, parents should not attempt to claim it as a tax credit, or attempt to qualify this expense for reimbursement via a flexible spending account. Please consult a qualified tax professional for further information.
Holidays: Math Genie will be closed on all major holidays. Your child will receive 48 classes per calendar year. Please refer to our yearly holiday calendar for scheduled closings (released every January). No makeup classes will be given for scheduled holiday closings.
Weather Disruptions: We will close all of our centers on all major weather related disruptions. We will try to give you a makeup for such disruptions. However, no credit or refund is due if you cannot attend the makeup class.

Make-up Policy

Make-up for missed classes.
If you plan to miss a class due to vacations, sickness or personal reasons, please let us know 24 hours in advance.
You can inform us by sending an email to {{ location }}@math-genie.com or visit support.mathgenie.com and create a support ticket.
A make-up class will only be provided with a 24 hour advance notice email or support ticket.
We will try our best to accommodate you, but make-ups are not guaranteed. No refunds or credits will be given for missed classes.
No Make-ups for No Shows.
If your child does not show up for class without canceling 24 hours in advance, No make-up class will be given. NO EXCEPTIONS.
Make-ups do not carry over.
You must schedule the make-up in the same month that your child missed the class. Make-ups do not carry over to the next month. This will allow us to provide everybody make-ups on a timely manner.
